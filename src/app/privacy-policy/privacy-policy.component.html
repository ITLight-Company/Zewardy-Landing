Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 18.01.2025

At Zewardy, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is protected. This Privacy Policy explains the types of data we collect, how we use it, how we store and protect it, and your rights regarding your personal data when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using our App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

  1. 1. Information We Collect

    We collect different types of data depending on your use of the App:

    • Personal Information: We may collect and store email addresses that you provide when registering or logging in through Firebase Authentication (Google account). This data is used solely for authentication and account-related purposes.
    • Usage Data: We collect anonymous data related to your use of the App, including:
      • Your quiz scores and interactions with the App,
      • Device information (e.g., device type, operating system),
      • IP addresses for security and usage analytics.
    • Payment Information: For payments or rewards, we do not store sensitive financial data (e.g., credit card numbers or bank account information) directly. Payments are processed by third-party services such as PayPal or gift cards. We do not store financial data except for transaction history necessary for account and payment verification. For more details on how your payment data is processed, please refer to the respective payment processor’s privacy policy (e.g., PayPal’s Privacy Policy).
    • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your experience and help us analyze the performance of the App. You can adjust your cookie preferences in your device settings.
  2. 2. Use of Data

    We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

    • To provide and improve the functionality of the App,
    • To personalize your quiz experience and content,
    • To serve targeted advertisements based on your usage and preferences,
    • To analyze usage trends and improve App performance,
    • To manage user authentication and account-related services using Firebase Authentication,
    • To process and confirm payments made through third-party platforms.
  3. 3. Payment Processing

    When you receive rewards or make payments through the App, third-party payment processors handle the financial transactions. These processors, such as PayPal or gift card services, are responsible for securely processing your payment information.

    We do not store sensitive financial data like credit card numbers or bank details. However, we retain basic transaction history, such as:

    • Payment date,
    • Amount,
    • Transaction status.

    This information is used to verify rewards, manage transactions, and ensure accurate record-keeping for users. For details on how your financial information is processed, please refer to the respective payment processor’s privacy policy.

  4. 4. Account Deletion

    To ensure the efficiency and security of the App, we may delete accounts that have not been accessed for more than 30 days. You can also request to delete your account at any time. Upon account deletion, we will remove your personal data unless we are required by law to retain it for a specific period.

  5. 5. Third-Party Services

    We use third-party services to enhance the functionality of our App. These services may include:

    • Firebase by Google for user authentication and data storage.
    • Payment processors (e.g., PayPal, gift cards) for processing transactions.
    • Advertising and analytics providers to serve targeted ads and analyze user behavior.

    Each third-party service has its own privacy practices, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies. For more details on Firebase, refer to the Firebase Privacy Policy.

  6. 6. Data Security

    We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal data using technical, physical, and organizational measures. These measures include:

    • Encryption of sensitive data during transmission,
    • Secure servers and controlled access to data,
    • Regular security audits.

    However, please be aware that no system is completely secure, and while we take all reasonable precautions to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

  7. 7. Your Rights

    Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

    • Access: You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
    • Correction: You can request to correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
    • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances.
    • Objection: You may object to the processing of your data in certain situations (e.g., for direct marketing purposes).
    • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used format.
    • Withdrawal of Consent: If you have given consent to process your data, you may withdraw it at any time.

    To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the contact details provided below.

  8. 8. Children's Privacy

    Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or maintain personal data from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under the age of 13, we will take immediate steps to remove that information.

  9. 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal obligations. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed about how we protect your data.

  10. 10. Limitation of Liability

    To the maximum extent permitted by law, Zewardy and its affiliates, officers, and employees shall not be held liable for any indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages arising out of or in connection with your use of the App, including but not limited to damages arising from loss of data, loss of profits, or any other financial losses.

  11. 11. Dispute Resolution and Governing Law

    This Privacy Policy shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of Poland and applicable laws of the European Union. Any disputes arising from the use of this Privacy Policy or the App shall be resolved through arbitration or in the competent courts in Poland.

  12. Contact Us

    If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

    Email: contact@zewardy.com
