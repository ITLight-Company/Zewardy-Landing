Privacy Policy
Effective Date: 21.11.2024
At Zewardy, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains the types of data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our mobile application (“App”). By using our App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

  1. Information We Collect:
    1. Personal Information: We may collect and store email addresses that you provide or those linked to your Google account when you log in through Firebase Authentication. This data is used solely for authentication and account-related purposes.
    2. Usage Data: We collect anonymous data related to how you use the App, including your quiz scores, interactions, and device information.
  2. Use of Data:
    1. To provide and improve the functionality of the App.
    2. To personalize your quiz experience.
    3. To serve targeted advertisements based on your usage and preferences.
    4. To analyze trends and improve the performance of the App.
    5. To manage user authentication and account-related services using Firebase Authentication.
  3. Third-Party Services:

    We use Firebase by Google for user authentication and data storage. Firebase securely stores email addresses and other account-related data as required for the functionality of our App. For more details, refer to the Firebase Privacy Policy.

  4. Data Security:

    We take the security of your data seriously and employ reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, please note that no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

  5. Your Rights:

    Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can also object to the processing of your data in certain situations.

  6. Children's Privacy:

    Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect or maintain personal data from children. If you believe that we have collected personal information from a child, please contact us, and we will take appropriate action to remove such data.
